Founded in 2019, Soglam has been championing clean beauty and lifestyle products for over 2 years now. Born out of a desire to see real change in the way we treat ourselves and the world around us, Soglam has fast become a trusted and ethical source in this field. we are driven to provide you with an exciting product range that reflects our uncompromising values.

Soglam beauty products are lovingly handmade in small batches at our studio in Beirut, Lebanon to ensure the freshness of the precious antioxidants in the natural oils, butters and extracts. Our products are vegan, cruelty free and eco-friendly.

We design and make each product carefully by hand and with a lot of love and care.
